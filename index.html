What is BukSU Save Parking

BUKSU Save Parking is a smart and simple mobile application designed to help students, faculty, and visitors of Bukidnon State University (BUKSU) easily save and locate their parked vehicles using real-time location tools. Whether you're parked in a busy lot or returning after class, never lose track of your car again!

Who is it for?

BUKSU Save Parking is designed for Bukidnon State University Students, Staff, Faculty Members, and Campus Visitors who often forget where they parked and need a convenient, stress-free way to save and locate their vehicle using reliable tools like GPS, Compass, and Maps.

Why Use It?

BUKSU Save Parking helps you never lose track of your car again, saving valuable time walking around campus by using smart technologies like GPS, Compass, and Maps all thoughtfully designed to meet the specific needs of the Bukidnon State University community.
